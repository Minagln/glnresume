The project owner sought a system that would streamline the purchase of cars through installments. To achieve this goal, I conducted user interviews, analyzed competitor offerings, and created a sitemap and task flow to map out the user journey. With a validated task flow in hand, I then proceeded to develop wireframes and visual designs.

Currently, the public pages of the designed website are undergoing final approval by the employer before implementation.
